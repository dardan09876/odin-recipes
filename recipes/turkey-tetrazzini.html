Turkey Tetrazzini

Turkey Tetrazzini is a comforting and hearty casserole dish that combines tender pieces of turkey with a creamy, savory sauce, pasta, and a blend of vegetables. Often enjoyed as a post-holiday meal, it’s the perfect way to repurpose leftover turkey while creating a satisfying dish for the whole family. The dish typically includes cooked spaghetti or egg noodles mixed with a creamy sauce made from butter, flour, chicken broth, and cream, seasoned with garlic, onions, and a touch of parmesan for added flavor. It is then baked to golden perfection, with a crispy breadcrumb topping that adds a delightful crunch to each bite.

This dish can be easily customized with your favorite vegetables, such as mushrooms, peas, or bell peppers, and is often enhanced with grated cheese for an extra layer of richness. Turkey Tetrazzini is not only delicious but also a great make-ahead option, as it can be assembled and stored in the fridge or freezer, ready to be baked when you’re ready to serve. Whether you're looking for a cozy dinner or need to use up holiday leftovers, Turkey Tetrazzini is a classic choice that’s sure to please everyone at the table.

Ingredients (Per Serving)
  • 12 ounces dried linguine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter
  • 8 ounces button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1/4 cup white wine (Optional)
  • 2 cups chopped cooked turkey
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 3/4 cup reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds (Optional)

Steps
  1. Boil and drain the pasta, then transfer it to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Cook the vegetables in butter, then stir in the seasonings.
  3. Deglaze the pan with wine, if using.
  4. Stir in the turkey, soup, sour cream, and broth. Cook until hot and bubbly.
  5. Spoon the turkey mixture over pasta and sprinkle with cheese and almonds.
  6. Bake the turkey tetrazzini until hot and bubbly.
